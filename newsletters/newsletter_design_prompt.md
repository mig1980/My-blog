## Current Design System

### Color Palette
- **Background**: Black (#000000) and dark gray (#111111, #1a1a1a)
- **Primary Accent**: Purple (#a855f7, #7c3aed)
- **Success/Positive**: Green (#22c55e, #4ade80)
- **Negative**: Red (#ef4444)
- **Text**: White (#ffffff), light gray (#E0E0E0), medium gray (#9ca3af, #6b7280)
- **Borders**: Dark gray (#222222, #2a2a2a)

### Typography
- **Font Family**: System fonts (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, etc.)
- **Sizes**: Range from 12px (labels) to 40px (main metrics)
- **Weights**: 500, 600, 700 for emphasis
- **Styling**: Uppercase labels with letter-spacing, line-height 1.3-1.75

### Layout Principles
- **Max Width**: 680px container
- **Spacing**: Consistent padding (20px, 24px, 32px, 40px)
- **Grid**: Two and three-column layouts for metrics
- **Borders**: 1-3px accent borders on left side of cards
- **Alignment**: Center-aligned headers and metrics, left-aligned body text

### Email Compatibility
- Table-based layout for email client compatibility
- Outlook conditional comments
- Mobile-responsive with @media queries
- Inline CSS styling
